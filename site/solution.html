The Problem & The Solution

Every business is drowning in data it can't use.

Email, CRM, documents, meetings, financials — all disconnected, with no single source of truth. The average portfolio company runs 7+ disconnected data systems and has no unified operational intelligence layer.

The Problem

Four ways the old way breaks down.

Data silos

Even with an ERP in place, data stays siloed in the past and just out of reach.

Slow decisions

Executives wait days for reports that are already outdated when delivered.

Invisible leakage

Vendor overcharges, missed signals, wrong hires, lost deals — invisible until too late.

Manual everything

High-value people doing low-value work: copy-paste, status updates, formatting, tickets.

The Solution

One operating layer across your entire firm.

Epheros isn't software you buy and implement. It's an operating layer that sits across your entire firm and portfolio — ingesting, connecting, analyzing, and acting.

One-click actions

Every insight is paired with a next action, ready to execute.

Autonomous execution

Repetitive workflow execution without a ticket or a hand-off.

Social intelligence

Market, competitor, and talent signals delivered with context and a recommendation.

Self-improving

The platform maintains and improves itself as it learns your business — self-coding, self-healing.

Under The Surface

Not a vibe-coded AI wrapper.

Every employee device becomes part of a self-healing, decentralized compute mesh — while Epheros autonomously generates the exact digital tools, strategic intelligence, and pre-emptive workflows a company needs.

Personalized Intelligence

From the CEO's portfolio health score to the CFO's daily P&L validation, each role gets a dashboard built around what actually matters to their job.
