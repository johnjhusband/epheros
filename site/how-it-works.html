How It Works

Zero-friction setup. Epheros learns your business in hours, not months.

No IT ticket. No months-long rollout. Six phases, running in the background from the moment you accept the invite.

01

Browser install

User opens a private invite URL, creates a password, and accepts a 2MB desktop bridge download. No IT ticket required.

02

Automatic ingestion

Epheros scans devices, reads email (one-click consent), and ingests documents, SharePoint, Dropbox, and Box, creating encrypted device tunnels to balance GPU and storage company-side.

03

Avatar construction

Builds a user profile: role, workflow, tools, communication style, relationships, permissions, and access levels.

04

Gap detection

Maps current workflow using Lean and Six Sigma principles, comparing company vs. marketplace best-of-breed to surface the 20% of fixes that deliver 80% of the gains.

05

Live dashboard

A personalized, role-aware dashboard appears within hours. Every insight is validated, verified, and actionable.

06

Continuous learning

Every edit, click, and outcome trains Epheros. It improves its autonomy level per task type as accuracy compounds.
